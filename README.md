# WORK DAY SCHEDULER

The calendar application you were searching for to save events for each hour of a typical working day.

https://leopoldogurgel.github.io/Work-Day-Scheduler/

## DESCRIPTION

This is a calendar application in which you organize your time by defining your tasks throughout your day.

![App landing page](./assets/images/screenshot.jpg "App landing page")

## INSTRUCTIONS

This is a single page app. You will be presented with the curent date and time and a table dividing your working hours. It starts at 9am and ends at 5pm. The app will identify the current hour by painting it red, the past hours by paniting the gray nd the future ones by painting them green. Click on them to write down your activities and click on the save buttom so they can be saved. Done. Now, even if you close your browser, your schedule will be saved and brought back whenever you open the page.
